Walmart gift card balance



Walmart gift cards are a popular and versatile way for individuals to shop at Walmart, providing the flexibility to purchase a wide range of products. Keeping track of your walmart gift card balance is essential for a seamless shopping experience. Here's an overview of Walmart gift card balances, how to check them, and important details to know


How do I check my Walmart gift card balance activity?

You can check your Walmart gift card balance and activity in a few ways:

  • Online: Visit the walmart gift card balance website and navigate to the "Gift Cards" section. Click on "Check Gift Card Balance" and enter your gift card number and PIN (if required). You'll be able to view your balance and recent transactions.

  • Phone: Call Walmart's customer service. Follow the prompts to enter your gift card number and PIN (if required), and a representative will assist you with checking your balance and activity.

  • In-store: Visit a Walmart store and go to the customer service desk. Provide your gift card to the associate, and they'll be able to check your balance and activity for you.

  • Mobile app: If you have the Walmart mobile app, you can also check your gift card balance and activity within the app. Simply log in to your account, navigate to the "Gift Cards" section, and enter your gift card number and PIN (if required).


What are the Types of Walmart Gift Cards?


1. Physical/Standard Gift Cards:

  • Traditional plastic gift cards available in-store or online.
  • Can be used for in-store purchases at Walmart, Sam's Club, and affiliated locations.

2. Digital Gift Cards:

  • Electronic versions (eGift cards) delivered via email.
  • Can be used for online purchases on the Walmart website and Sam's Club.

3. Special Occasion Gift Cards:

  • Themed gift cards for events like birthdays, holidays, graduations, etc.
  • May have unique designs and specific packaging for the occasion.

4. Corporate Gift Cards:

  • Designed for business or bulk purchases.
  • Can be used for business-related expenses or as employee incentives.

5. Reloadable Gift Cards:

  • Some Walmart gift cards are reloadable for additional funds.
  • Useful for multiple transactions with the same card.

6. Walmart MoneyCard:

  • Prepaid debit card offered by Walmart.
  • Can be used for purchases wherever debit cards are accepted and is reloadable with funds.

Understanding the Activation Process for Walmart Gift Cards

Here's a general overview of the standard process for using Walmart gift cards:

1. Purchase:

  • You can buy a Walmart gift card from Walmart stores or online through the Walmart website.

2. Loading Funds:

  • Choose the amount you want to load onto the gift card, which can typically range from $5 to $500.

3. Checkout:

  • Complete the purchase transaction at the cashier or online, depending on where you bought the gift card.

4. Receiving the Card:

  • If it's a physical gift card, you'll receive a plastic card with a unique 16-digit card number and a 4-digit PIN.

5. Immediate Use:

  • Your Walmart gift card is ready for immediate use after purchase. You can use it for in-store or online purchases at Walmart.

FAQ's


Walmart Gift Cards

Walmart offers two types of gift cards: physical (tangible) cards with a PIN and online-only (e-cards) gift cards.


Where can I use my Walmart Gift Card?

Physical gift cards can be used in any United States or Puerto Rico Walmart store or Sam's Club, including parts of the facility that are managed by Walmart. They can also be used on walmart.com and samsclub.com (membership required). Online-only gift cards can only be used online at walmart.com or samsclub.com (membership required).


Can I reload my Gift Card?

No, your Gift Card is not reloadable.

What are the fees associated with the Gift Card?

The Walmart Visa Gift Card comes in various fixed denominations and variable denominations between $25-$500.


How do I purchase a Walmart Visa Gift Card?

You can purchase the Walmart Visa Gift Card in participating Walmart locations. For a location near you, please visit www.walmartgift.com.


What if I spent all of the funds on the Card?

Once all of the funds on the Card have been spent, the Card cannot be used for other purchases. If you are sure you will not return any products purchased with the Gift Card, we recommend you safely destroy the Card.
